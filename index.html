01 — THE INVESTIGATOR

From claims files to test plans

I spent over six years as a catastrophe claims adjuster, hurricanes, fires, hailstorms, and tornadoes. Investigating evidence and assessing risk in catastrophe insurance taught me how to spot flaws under pressure. It rewired my brain, and when I decided to shift into software, that mindset came with me.

Now at TalkTastic, I test our LLM app across every platform, from Mac and iPad to Pixel and Samsung. I focus on making sure prompts hit the mark, transcriptions are flawless, and regressions don't make it out the door. There’s nothing more satisfying than breaking something before a customer sees it, then giving the dev team the exact roadmap to fix it.

I'm happiest when I can prove something is broken before a customer ever sees it, then hand engineering a report clear enough to fix it once.
